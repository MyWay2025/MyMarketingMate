Our Story

Founded in 2025, Retail Cafe is an independent coffee roaster and eatery. We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients and roasting our own beans each week. Our chefs create menus that pair harmoniously with our coffee selection, ensuring every visit delights the senses.
